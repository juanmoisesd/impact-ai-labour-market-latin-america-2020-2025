# Impact of Artificial Intelligence on the Labour Market in Latin America (2020-2025)

## Description

Dataset on the impact of artificial intelligence on the labour market in Latin America (2020-2025). Includes automation indices, job displacement projections, emerging occupations, and governmental policy interventions.

## Repository Structure

- `automation_by_sector.csv` — Automation indices by economic sector
- `labour_displacement.csv` — Job displacement projections
- `emerging_occupations.csv` — New AI-driven occupations
- `labour_policies.csv` — Government policy interventions
- `analysis.ipynb` — Reproducible analysis in Python
